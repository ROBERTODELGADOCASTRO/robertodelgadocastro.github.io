Biography

Roberto Delgado Castro is an academic and researcher specialized in data science, applied econometrics, energy analysis, and predictive modeling. His work focuses on the application of advanced quantitative methods to economic, financial, and energy-related problems, combining academic rigor with a practical, decision-oriented approach.

Roberto Delgado Castro es un académico e investigador especializado en ciencia de datos, econometría aplicada, análisis energético y modelos predictivos.
